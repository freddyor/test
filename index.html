We all have powerful new technology in our hands.
Instead of inspiring negativity, division and hatred, it is time we use technology to inspire interest and enthusiasm in Britain and its heritage - for the next generations to come.
My name is Freddy. I am not a historian or a scholar, but I know how to engage a modern, younger audience like few historians are capable of.
This site is solely my project, built and coded by me. It is starting in York, where I am based, but I aim to cover the entire of Britain at some point. It will be a monumental task.
